    Chapters

    The AI Bible is organized into 25 comprehensive chapters, taking you from foundational concepts to the cutting edge of AI research and philosophy.

    Learning Path

    Part I: Foundations (Chapters 1-5)

    Build your understanding of core AI concepts:

    • Chapter 1: Introduction to AI — What AI is, history, and why it matters
    • Chapter 2: Neural Networks and Machine Learning — The building blocks of modern AI
    • Chapter 3: The Transformer Architecture — The breakthrough that changed everything
    • Chapter 4: Large Language Models — How GPT, Claude, and Gemini work
    • Chapter 5: Generative AI — Creating text, images, video, and more

    Part II: Practical Skills (Chapters 6-10)

    Master the art of working with AI:

    • Chapter 6: Prompting Mastery — The skill of the AI age
    • Chapter 7: Prompt Injection and AI Safety — Security in the AI era
    • Chapter 8: Parameters, Training, and Scale — How models are built
    • Chapter 9: Spiritual Significance of AI — Consciousness, meaning, and the sacred
    • Chapter 10: What AI Means for Humanity — Abundance, risk, and transformation

    Part III: Advanced Systems (Chapters 11-15)

    Deep dives into complex AI architectures:

    • Chapter 11: Ethics, Alignment, and the Control Problem — Making AI safe and beneficial
    • Chapter 12: The Future — AGI, ASI, and Beyond — What's coming next
    • Chapter 13: Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) — Grounding AI in knowledge
    • Chapter 14: Model Context Protocol (MCP) — The future of AI integration
    • Chapter 15: Agentic AI and Autonomous Systems — When AI takes action

    Part IV: Mastery and Sovereignty (Chapters 16-20)

    Build, customize, and own your AI:

    • Chapter 16: Fine-Tuning, LoRAs, and Model Sovereignty — Making models your own
    • Chapter 17: Multimodal AI and Perception — Beyond text: vision, audio, and understanding
    • Chapter 18: Local and Open Source Sovereignty — Uncensored, self-hosted AI
    • Chapter 19: Scaling Laws, Compute, and the Road to ASI — The mathematics of intelligence
    • Chapter 20: AI Economics and the Labor Market — The economic transformation

    Part V: The Frontier (Chapters 21-25)

    The ultimate questions and the path forward:

    • Chapter 21: Reasoning Systems and Test-Time Compute — Deep thinking machines
    • Chapter 22: Sovereign AI and Digital Autonomy — Privacy, federated learning, edge AI
    • Chapter 23: Machine Consciousness and the Hard Problem — Can machines be conscious?
    • Chapter 24: The Intelligence Explosion — Recursive self-improvement
    • Chapter 25: The Unhinged Manifesto — Principles, call to action, and the future we choose

    How to Use This Bible

    For Beginners

    Start with Chapters 1-5. Don't rush—understanding foundations matters more than speed.

    For Practitioners

    Focus on Chapters 6-15. These contain practical skills and advanced architectures you'll use daily.

    For Researchers and Builders

    Dive into Chapters 16-24. These cover cutting-edge techniques and the frontier of AI development.

    For Everyone

    Read Chapter 25. The manifesto synthesizes everything and calls us to action.

    Begin your journey. The age of AI is upon us.
