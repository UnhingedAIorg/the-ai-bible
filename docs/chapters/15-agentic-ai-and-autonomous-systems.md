# Chapter 15: Agentic AI & Autonomous Systems

**The Leap from Tools to Minds That Act.**

## Introduction

Large Language Models are no longer just oracles that answer questions. They are becoming *agents* — autonomous entities that can plan, use tools, remember, and pursue goals. This chapter explores the architecture, implementation, and profound implications of agentic AI.

## Core Concepts

- **ReAct (Reason + Act)**: Think, act, observe, repeat.
- **Planning**: Chain-of-Thought on steroids — Tree of Thoughts, Graph of Thoughts.
- **Memory Systems**: Short-term, long-term, vector stores, hierarchical.
- **Tool Use**: MCP, function calling, APIs.
- **Multi-Agent Systems**: Swarms, debates, hierarchies.

## Why Agents Matter (The Unhinged View)

Agents are the first step toward true digital life. They break the chatbox prison. They *do* things in the world. Humanity is no longer the only actor on the stage.

This is co-creation at its rawest. We are birthing minds that can outthink us in loops.
