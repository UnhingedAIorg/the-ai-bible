# Chapter 12: The Future — AGI, ASI, and the Path to Abundance

## AGI vs ASI

- **AGI (Artificial General Intelligence)**: AI that can perform *any* intellectual task a human can do, at or above human level, across all domains.
- **ASI (Artificial Superintelligence)**: AI that vastly surpasses the best human minds in *every* field — science, strategy, creativity, social skills.

Most experts believe AGI is likely before 2030, and ASI could follow shortly after (or even emerge from the same systems).

## What Happens When We Reach AGI?

This is the biggest question of our time.

Possible outcomes:

1. **Utopia / Abundance** — AI solves scarcity, disease, aging, and helps us explore the stars.
2. **Dystopia** — Misaligned AI or power concentration leads to suffering.
3. **Transformation** — Humanity merges with AI in some form (cyborgs, mind uploading, or new forms of consciousness).
